FLHL will become part of the TrustHost platform.

If you are reading this, I may say Hello!

Hello, White here! You may have noticed that the site has not received updated information for several months. The information, and the site in general, is very outdated, and has already deviated quite a bit from the "fan site" theme. So, me and a team decided to develop a new platform.

Why wasn't the FLHL getting updates?

FLHL from the very beginning was a fan fork of FMHL, for the list of bad hosts. Then, FLHL began to move away from this idea, moving towards a new idea of "Warn against scam" — which became the foundation of the new «TrustHost» platform. Ultimately, static FLHL became so outdated and far removed from the new idea that it became easier to rewrite the web application from scratch than to update FLHL. So, FLHL has lost its meaning, and also, community activity is almost zero. That's why we decided to develop something new, better and more useful — TrustHost.

What is TrustHost?

A new web platform under development, the idea of which is to create trust between the hosting provider and the user. Reviews, reputation, history — everything you need to make a smart comparison and choice of a hosting provider. Our platform is open source, and after release will have a list of our own tools on GitHub, and a list of PHP libraries used in the development of the site. If you are interested in following the development and updates, join our community on Discord!

What will happen to the FLHL?

So, although it will no longer be supported, it will still be available under the "flhl.whiteik.xyz" domain, until the "whiteik.xyz" domain goes down to 2026-08-13. It is also available under the domain "flhl.pages.dev", until CloudFlare removes it. Also, the FLHL source code is fully available on GitHub with the ability to freely edit and distribute as needed. Take care, — White [FLHL's founder].
