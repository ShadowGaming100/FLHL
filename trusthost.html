Why did FLHL stop receiving updates?

FLHL began as a fork of FMHL focused on bad hosts, then evolved toward a scam-warning mission. The static HTML approach became too difficult to maintain as the scope grew. Rather than patch an outdated codebase, the decision was made to build a proper platform from scratch.

What is TrustHost?

A web platform designed to create transparency between hosting providers and users. It will feature reviews, reputation tracking, and historical data. The platform will be fully open-source, with all tools and libraries published on GitHub.

Will FLHL remain accessible?

Yes. FLHL will stay available at flhl.whiteik.xyz until the domain expires on 2026-08-13, and at flhl.pages.dev on Cloudflare Pages. The source code is fully available on GitHub for anyone to fork.
