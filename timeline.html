FLHL Timeline

Key milestones from development to retirement.

Jan 2025

Development started

WhiTeik24 began work on FLHL because FMHL's strict listing rules were blocking many hosts.

Apr 2025

Public release

FLHL launched as a structured website with categorized hosts (Low / New / Bad), ratings, and specs.

25 Jan 2026

Archive system added

Git commit snapshots were introduced to preserve every version of the site.

19 Jun 2026

Retirement

FLHL was retired and migrated to TrustHost. All archives remain available on the Archive page.

Total lifespan: 1 year, 5 months. 32 hosts documented across 19 archived snapshots.
